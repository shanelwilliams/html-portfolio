The Best Movies According to Shanel

My top 3 movies of all time.

Labyrinth

Quirky, crazy, and overall a timeless classic that I will love forever.

Wicked Part 1 & 2

Mindblowing to see the back story of both Glinda and the wicked withch of the west. I feel like the entire story of the Wizard of Oz makes so much more sense. I was shocked at the end with all the twists and turns.

Zootopia 2

This kids movie is actually very funny and like many kid movies has a lot of jokes or scenes that only adults would understand. I really enjoy watching this with the kids.
